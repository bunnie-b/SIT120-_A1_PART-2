1. Remember the process of scoring points on your credit/debit card purchases? Our system uses the same concept.



2. With us, you simply have to plant a tree of your liking! Once it’s planted, you can register it’s details on the TREE REGISTRY PAGE. Then, you can go on and purchase an oxygen tracking kit from our referred stores. The kit will have instructions on how to set up the equipment. It’s just to help you track the oxygen produced.



3. Once your equipment is all set up, you will be able to view and record the data as required. Every 2 weeks, log on to your profile and update the oxygen produced by the planted tree.



4. Every time you update your data, you will see a bar on the left hand side, indicating the rewards meter. This will increase as the oxygen levels increase. These rewards can be used at your local stores or supermarkets to get various discounts!
